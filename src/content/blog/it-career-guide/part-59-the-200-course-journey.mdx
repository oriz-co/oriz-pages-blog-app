> [!NOTE]
> **"I am interested in everything. Where do I start?"**
>
> If you have the ambition to become a true polymath (an *Omnimath*)—someone who understands complex multi-agent AI systems, reads financial balance sheets, edits video, negotiates like a CEO, and plays the piano—you need a sequence. 
>
> You cannot learn 200 things simultaneously. But you *can* learn 200 things in a precise, interleaved order where each skill acts as a force multiplier for the next. This updated roadmap heavily emphasizes **2026's newer tech (MCP, Agentic AI, Local LLMs)** alongside foundational disciplines.

---
