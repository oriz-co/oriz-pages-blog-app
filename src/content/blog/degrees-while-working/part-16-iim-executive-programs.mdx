## 1. IIM Ahmedabad — Blended Post Graduate Programme (BPGP)

IIM Ahmedabad offers a highly prestigious **Blended Post Graduate Programme in 
Management (BPGP)**, structured as an online MBA for working professionals.

### Program Structure
- **Duration:** 2 Years.
- **Delivery:** Blended structure. Approximately 80% of classes are synchronous 
  online sessions, with the remaining 20% conducted during mandatory physical 
  modules on the IIMA campus.
- **Degree Awarded:** Master of Business Administration (MBA) from IIM Ahmedabad.

### Admission & Fees
- **Eligibility:** Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of **3 years** of full-time 
  work experience post-graduation.
- **Selection Process:** Score in the **IIMA Admission Test (IAT)**, CAT, GMAT, or 
  GRE, followed by a personal interview round.
- **Total Fees:** **₹20,00,000** (excl. travel, dining, and stay during campus visits).

---

## 2. IIM Bangalore — PGPEM (Post Graduate Programme in Enterprise Management)

The PGPEM is a weekend-only Executive MBA program offered on-campus at IIM 
Bangalore, specifically for professionals working in the city's tech hubs.

### Program Structure
- **Duration:** 2 Years (4 Semesters).
- **Delivery:** Friday afternoon and Saturday classes on the IIMB campus. 
  Requires candidates to reside or travel to Bangalore weekly.
- **Alumni Status:** Granted full access to the BITSian-equivalent IIMB Alumni Association.

### Admission & Fees
- **Eligibility:** Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of **3 years** of post-qualification 
  work experience.
- **Selection:** CAT, GMAT, GRE, or the proprietary **IIMB Test**, followed by a personal 
  interview.
- **Total Fees:** **₹20,11,000** (payable in quarterly installments).

---

## 3. IIM Kozhikode — EPGP (Executive Post Graduate Programme)

IIM Kozhikode was one of the early pioneers of technology-enabled executive education 
in India. Its EPGP program is highly sought after by IT/Software professionals.

### Program Structure
- **Duration:** 2 Years.
- **Delivery:** Live online classes broadcast through interactive technology platforms 
  on evenings/weekends, coupled with short campus immersion modules.
- **Admission Test:** **EMAT (Executive Management Aptitude Test)** conducted online by 
  IIMK, or CAT/GMAT/GRE.

### Admission & Fees
- **Eligibility:** Graduation with 50%+ marks, and a minimum of **3 years** of work experience.
- **Total Fees:** ~₹14,00,000.

---

## 4. Why the 3-Year Experience Requirement Exists

All top-tier IIM blended/executive programs mandate a minimum of **3 years of work experience**. 
This is because:
1. **Case-Based Learning:** IIM pedagogy heavily relies on case studies. Students must have 
   practical corporate exposure to contribute to discussions.
2. **Peer Learning:** Classes consist of team leads, managers, and consultants. Having a class 
   with similar professional tenure enhances the cohort experience.
3. **Corporate Value:** Executive MBAs are valued by employers specifically when combined with 
   proven operational experience.

---

## Strategic Advice for a TCS Engineer (B.Tech, Age 23, TCS Bhubaneswar)

If you are a 23-year-old engineer with 2 years of experience at TCS:
- **Patience is Key:** You currently do not meet the 3-year experience criteria. Focus on 
  getting your third year of experience while preparing for tests like the **IAT** or **EMAT**.
- **Location Constraints:** Programs like IIMB PGPEM require on-campus weekend presence, 
  which is difficult if you are based in Bhubaneswar. Opt for **IIM Ahmedabad BPGP** or 
  **IIM Kozhikode EPGP**, which are mostly online and only require 1 or 2 weeks of travel 
  to campus per year.
- **ROI Analysis:** Spending ₹14L to ₹20L is a massive investment. Ensure that your career 
  goal is to transition into Product Management, Consulting, or Corporate Strategy. If you 
  want to remain in core software engineering/coding, a BITS WILP or IIT online M.Tech 
  at a fraction of the cost is a better choice.
