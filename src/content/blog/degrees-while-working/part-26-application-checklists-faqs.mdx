## 1. Master Application Document Checklist

Prepare these documents in digital format (PDF/JPEG, usually under 200KB or 1MB) 
before beginning any online application process:

- **Identity Proof:** Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, or Passport.
- **Academic Qualifications:**
  - Class 10 Marksheet & Passing Certificate.
  - Class 12 Marksheet & Passing Certificate.
  - Semester-wise Graduation Marksheets & Consolidated Marksheet.
  - Provisional Degree Certificate or Original Degree Certificate.
- **Professional Details:**
  - Appointment Letter/Joining Letter from current employer (e.g., TCS).
  - Work Experience Certificate (from previous companies, if any).
  - Salary Slips (usually the last 3 months).
  - Official Work Email ID (for verification of employment status).
- **Consent Forms:**
  - Employer No-Objection Certificate (NOC) or BITS-style **Mentor Consent Form**.
- **Financial Aid (if applying for waivers):**
  - Income Certificate issued by competent revenue authority (valid for current year).

## 2. Core FAQs for Working Professionals

### Q: Can I pursue two degrees simultaneously in India?
**Yes.** Under the **UGC Notification (April 2022)**, a student can pursue two academic 
programs simultaneously:
1. One in full-time physical mode and another in Open and Distance Learning (ODL)/Online mode.
2. Two ODL/Online programs simultaneously.
*Note: Ensure class/exam timings do not overlap.*

### Q: Are online degrees valid for UPSC or government job applications?
**Yes.** The UGC-DEB has officially notified that degrees/diplomas awarded through ODL 
and Online modes by recognized Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs) are treated as 
equivalent to degrees awarded through regular classroom modes for all corporate and 
government employment.

### Q: How do online proctored exams work?
For 100% online programs, exams are taken from home:
- You must use a laptop/desktop with a working webcam, microphone, and stable internet.
- An AI software and a human proctor monitor your screen, face, and room audio 
  in real-time.
- Any suspicious movement (looking away, talking, opening other tabs) flag alerts 
  that can lead to disqualification.

### Q: How do I get an NOC from a service company like TCS?
1. Open the **Ultimatix** portal.
2. Navigate to the **HR/Learning Policies** section.
3. Apply under **"Request for Higher Education NOC"**.
4. You will need to fill in details: University name, degree name, duration, and class timings.
5. Once your Project Manager and HR partner approve, a digital NOC is issued to your official email.

### Q: What happens if I change my job mid-semester during a WILP program?
For BITS WILP:
- You must submit a **Change of Employment Form** to the BITS administration portal.
- You must provide the joining letter of your new company and get a new **Mentor Consent Form** 
  signed by a senior colleague at your new workplace to maintain your registration.
