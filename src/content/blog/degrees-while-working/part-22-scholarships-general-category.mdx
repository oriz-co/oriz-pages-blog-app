## 1. National Scholarship Portal (NSP) Schemes

For General Category (Unreserved) students, scholarships are primarily **merit-cum-means** 
or EWS (Economically Weaker Section) based.

### Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship (CSSS)
- **Eligibility:** Students who are in the top 20th percentile of their state board in 
  Class 12, pursuing regular higher studies, and have a family income under **₹4.5 Lakhs/year**.
- **Amount:** ₹12,000/year for undergraduate and ₹20,000/year for postgraduate courses.
- **Official Portal:** https://scholarships.gov.in

---

## 2. State-Specific Schemes: Odisha focus

For candidates residing or studying in Odisha (like Chirag):

### A. e-Medhabruti Scholarship
- **Eligibility:** Merit-based scholarship offered by the Higher Education Department 
  of Odisha. General Category students are fully eligible.
- **Income Limit:** Annual family income must not exceed **₹6,000,000** (₹6 Lakhs).
- **Amount:**
  - **PG Merit:** ₹10,000/year.
  - **Technical/Professional Merit:** ₹20,000/year.

### B. Gopabandhu Sikhya Sahayata Yojana
- **Eligibility:** Students pursuing PG, technical, or professional courses whose 
  families are in distressed circumstances (e.g., HIV/AIDS affected, single mother, 
  insolvent). General category is fully eligible.
- **Amount:** Up to ₹20,000/year towards tuition.

---

## 3. Institutional Fee Waivers (The IIT Madras Example)

Some online programs build robust financial aid directly into their fee structures:

### IIT Madras BS Program Merit-cum-Means Waiver:
- **Family Income < ₹1 Lakh/year:** **75% fee waiver** (for all categories including General).
- **Family Income between ₹1L - ₹5 Lakhs/year:** **50% fee waiver**.
- **SC/ST Category or General-EWS (with income limits):** Additional waivers up to 100% 
  for foundation levels.
- **Corporate Sponsor Scholarships:** Partners like Tata AIA and Verizon offer full 
  scholarships to meritorious students in the Diploma and Degree levels based on GPA.

---

## 4. Corporate & NGO Scholarships

- **Tata Trusts Scholarships:** Offers need-based and merit-based scholarship grants for 
  postgraduate studies in India. Application opens annually around September.
- **TCS Higher Education Support:** Employees at TCS should look under Ultimatix 
  "Corporate Learning" for internal sponsorship programs or partial tuition reimbursements 
  for courses matching their current project delivery needs.

---

## The Key Document: Income Certificate

For General Category candidates, the **Income Certificate** is the single most critical 
document.
- It must be issued by a competent revenue authority (like a Tehsildar or Revenue Inspector).
- In Odisha, you can apply online for an Income Certificate via the **e-District Odisha** 
  portal (edistrict.odisha.gov.in) using your Aadhaar card and family income details.
- Ensure the certificate is updated for the current financial year before applying on the 
  NSP portal.
