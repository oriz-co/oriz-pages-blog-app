## 3. Key Decision Matrix

To simplify your choice, use this quick selector based on your primary career constraint:

- **Constraint: Lowest Possible Cost (UG/PG Degree)**
  - *Recommendation:* IGNOU MBA/MCA, DU SOL, or SWAYAM/NPTEL for free course credits.
- **Constraint: Maximum Professional Technical Brand in India**
  - *Recommendation:* BITS Pilani WILP M.Tech or IIT Kanpur eMasters.
- **Constraint: Transition to US/MNC Product Companies**
  - *Recommendation:* Georgia Tech OMSCS or UT Austin MSCSO.
- **Constraint: Shift to General/Consulting Management**
  - *Recommendation:* IIM Ahmedabad BPGP or IIM Kozhikode EPGP (after gaining 3 years of work experience).
