## 2. 5 Immediate Action Steps

### Step 1: Choose Your Program
Narrow down your choices to exactly two programs (one primary, one backup). Do not 
overcomplicate by applying to too many options.

### Step 2: Download the Official Brochure
Visit the official links listed in Part 27 and download the latest information 
brochure. Check for recent fee revisions or changes in eligibility policies.

### Step 3: Audit Your Study Schedule
Be honest with yourself: Can you allocate **10–12 hours every week** to self-study? 
Ensure your current project deliverables allow this breathing room.

### Step 4: Gather Your Documents
Create a dedicated folder on your computer containing clear, scanned copies of 
your graduation documents, ID proofs, and salary slips.

### Step 5: Secure Managerial and HR Approvals
If your program requires a Mentor Consent Form or a formal NOC (like BITS WILP 
or IIT Hyderabad), discuss it with your project manager and apply for the NOC 
via the HR portal (e.g., Ultimatix for TCS).

---

## Final Encouragement

Pursuing a degree while working is a marathon, not a sprint. It requires discipline, 
sacrificing some weekend leisure time, and constant focus. However, the reward—graduating 
with a Tier-1 degree from institutions like BITS Pilani or IIT while earning a continuous 
salary and accumulating active industry experience—is one of the most powerful career 
acceleraators available in modern India.

Good luck on your educational journey!
