## 1. IIT Madras BS Programs (Data Science & Electronic Systems)

IIT Madras was the first IIT to offer a fully-fledged, UGC-recognized online 
degree program. These programs are structured with multiple exit options.

**Official Portal:** https://study.iitm.ac.in

### The Multi-Exit Structure
Both the BS in Data Science & Applications and the BS in Electronic Systems 
allow students to exit at various levels with a credential:

1. **Foundation Level (approx. 1 Year):** Foundation Certificate.
2. **Diploma Level (approx. 2 Years):** One or two Diplomas.
3. **BSc Degree Level (approx. 3 Years):** BSc Degree from IIT Madras.
4. **BS Degree Level (approx. 4 Years):** BS Degree (4-year undergraduate).

### BS in Data Science & Applications

#### Eligibility
- Anyone who has passed Class 12 or equivalent.
- No age limit or stream restrictions.
- Ideal for B.Tech CSE graduates who want a formal specialization from IIT.

#### Fees (Data Science)
- **Foundation Level:** ~₹32,000 (8 courses)
- **Diploma Level:** ~₹1,10,000 (12 courses)
- **BSc Degree Level:** ~₹1,00,000 (9 courses)
- **BS Degree Level:** ~₹1,00,000 (9 courses)
- **Total Fees (BS Level):** ~₹3,42,000 - ₹3,60,000 (depending on electives).

### BS in Electronic Systems

#### Eligibility
- Class 12 with Physics and Mathematics.
- No age limit.

#### Fees (Electronic Systems)
- **Foundation Level:** ~₹80,000
- **Diploma Level:** ~₹1,56,000
- **BS Degree Level:** ~₹1,08,000
- **Total Fees:** ~₹3,44,000.

### The Qualifier Exam Pathway
Unlike JEE, admission is through a **Qualifier Exam**:
1. Apply online and pay the application fee (~₹3,000).
2. Complete 4 weeks of online coursework (Math, Statistics, English, Python).
3. Attempt weekly assignments (must score above threshold to qualify for exam).
4. Sit for the in-person proctored Qualifier Exam.
5. If you pass, you are admitted to the Foundation Level.

**TCS Engine Working Professional Suitability:**
- **Time commitment:** 10–15 hours per week per course.
- **Examinations:** In-person quizzes and final exams at designated cities 
  (Bhubaneswar has active centres).
- **Placement Cell:** IIT Madras provides an active Industry Interaction Cell 
  (IIC) for placement support to students reaching the diploma/degree level.

## 2. IIT Kanpur eMasters Programs

IIT Kanpur's eMasters programs are postgraduate executive programs designed for 
working professionals who want an IIT credential without quitting their job.

**Official Portal:** https://emasters.iitk.ac.in

### Key Specializations for CSE/IT
- Cyber Security
- Communication Systems
- Business Finance / Public Policy
- Data Science and Business Analytics
- Financial Technology and Alternative Regulatory Systems

### Program Features
- **Duration:** 1 to 3 years (flexible structure).
- **Format:** 100% online learning with weekend live interactive sessions.
- **GATE Score:** **Not required**.
- **Alumni Status:** Graduates receive official IIT Kanpur Alumni status, complete 
  with email address and access to campus events/networking.

### Eligibility
- A 4-year Bachelor's degree (B.Tech/B.E./BS) or a Master's degree in 
  Engineering, Science, or Computer Applications with at least 55% marks (or 5.5/10 CPI).
- Minimum **2 years of professional work experience**.

### Fee Structure
- **Total Fees:** ~₹8,00,000 to ₹9,20,000 (payable in installments).
- **Financing:** EMI and educational loan tie-ups are available through partner banks.

## 3. IIT Bombay CEP (Continuing Education Programme)

IIT Bombay's CEP offers short-term and long-term certificates rather than full 
degrees. They are highly targeted for industry skill upgrades.

**Official Portal:** https://www.cep.iitb.ac.in

### Program Types
- **Short-Term Courses:** 2 to 5 days intensive bootcamps.
- **Long-Term/Executive Programs:** 3 to 12 months online/hybrid courses in 
  AI/ML, Data Science, Fintech, and Management.
- **Target Audience:** Working professionals seeking high-impact, short-term 
  credentials from IIT Bombay.

### Admission & Fees
- **Selection:** Profile-based selection (Resume + Statement of Purpose).
- **Fees:** Range from ₹15,000 for short courses to ₹1,50,000+ for year-long 
  executive certificates.

## 4. IIT Hyderabad Executive M.Tech Programs

IIT Hyderabad offers Executive M.Tech programs for working professionals. These 
are coursework-heavy master's degrees.

**Official Portal:** https://www.iith.ac.in

### Key Specializations
- Data Science
- Network and Information Security
- EV Technology
- Computational Mechanics

### Admission & Fees
- **Eligibility:** B.Tech/B.E. in CSE/ECE/EE with 60%+ marks, and a minimum of 2 
  years of relevant industry experience.
- **GATE Score:** **Not required**.
- **Selection:** Written test and/or interview conducted by the department.
- **Fees:** ~₹6,00,000 to ₹9,00,000 depending on credit registration.
- **Format:** Online evening/weekend classes, with some lab requirements.

## FAQs

### Q: Are these programs valid for government jobs in India?
Yes. IIT degrees/diplomas approved by the respective Senate/Board of Governors 
are recognized. However, note that "eMasters" is a post-graduate certificate, 
not a traditional "M.Tech" degree. If a government notification strictly asks for 
an "M.Tech," the eMasters may not qualify; you should opt for BITS WILP or 
traditional M.Tech.

### Q: Does TCS reimburse the fees for these programs?
TCS has learning policies that reimburse specific certifications (like NPTEL or 
AWS/Azure/Google Cloud). Full degree reimbursement is typically reserved for 
pre-approved executive or sponsored programs. Always check the current policy 
on the TCS Ultimatix portal or consult your HR partner.

### Q: Can I apply for a PhD after completing an eMasters from IIT Kanpur?
No, an eMasters is not a traditional Master of Technology (M.Tech) or Master of 
Science (M.S.) research degree. It does not fulfill the 10+2+4+2 academic year 
requirement typically mandated for doctoral research in India. If a PhD is your 
end goal, consider a part-time M.Tech or direct PhD route.
