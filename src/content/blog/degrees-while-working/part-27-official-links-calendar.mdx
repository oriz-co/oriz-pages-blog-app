## 2. Standard Admission Cycles Calendar

Most universities in India offer admissions in **two major cycles** per academic year:

### A. Monsoon / July Intake
- **Admissions Open:** March – May
- **Application Deadlines:** June – July
- **Classes Begin:** August
- **Exams Conducted:** November – December
- **Applicable To:** Almost all Indian open universities (IGNOU, state open universities), 
  BITS Pilani WILP, IIT Madras BS, and standard private universities.

### B. Winter / January Intake
- **Admissions Open:** September – October
- **Application Deadlines:** November – December
- **Classes Begin:** February
- **Exams Conducted:** May – June
- **Applicable To:** IGNOU, BITS Pilani WILP (second cycle), private online universities.

---

## 3. US Universities (OMSCS / MSCSO) Admissions

If you are applying for international online programs (like Georgia Tech or UT Austin), 
the timeline shifts to fit the US academic calendar:

- **Fall Term (August start):** Applications open in January; close around March 15.
- **Spring Term (January start):** Applications open in June; close around August 15.
- **Decision Timelines:** Usually takes 2–3 months to receive admission letters after 
  submitting portfolios.
