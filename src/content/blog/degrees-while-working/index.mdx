# Degrees While Working India: The Ultimate Master Index

> **Who this guide is for:** B.Tech graduates working full-time in
> India — at TCS, Infosys, Wipro, or any other company — who want
> to upgrade their qualifications without quitting their jobs,
> without spending a fortune, and without losing their income.

> **Special focus:** General category candidates who cannot rely on
> reservation-based fee waivers and need options that work on merit
> and affordability alone.

---

## Why This Guide Exists

You graduated with a B.Tech. You got a job. You are earning a salary.
But you feel stuck. The next level — senior engineer, team lead,
architect, manager, researcher — seems to require something more.
Maybe it is an M.Tech. Maybe it is an MBA. Maybe it is a PhD with
a stipend that lets you earn while you study.

The problem is that most guides online either tell you to quit your job,
push expensive courses, or do not explain the actual processes.

**This guide is different.** It covers every legitimate path for a
general-category, working B.Tech graduate in India.
