## 1. Georgia Tech — OMSCS (Online Master of Science in Computer Science)

Georgia Tech's OMSCS is widely regarded as the gold standard of affordable online 
postgraduate technical education globally.

- **Rankings:** Georgia Tech consistently ranks in the top 10 for Computer Science 
  in the US.
- **Cost:** ~$180 per credit hour. 30 credits (10 courses) are required, bringing 
  the total tuition cost to **~$6,500 - $8,000** (approx. ₹5.4 Lakhs - ₹6.7 Lakhs 
  depending on forex rates).
- **Specializations:** Machine Learning, Computing Systems, Interactive Intelligence, 
  and Human-Computer Interaction.
- **Admission Requirements:**
  - A Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a highly quantitative field.
  - Recommended undergraduate GPA of **3.0 or higher** on a 4.0 scale.
  - **3 Letters of Recommendation** (academic and/or professional).
  - English proficiency exam (TOEFL score 90+ or IELTS 7.5+).
  - **No GRE required**.

---

## 2. UT Austin — MSCSO (Master of Science in Computer Science Online)

Similar to Georgia Tech, the University of Texas at Austin offers a top-tier online 
master's degree program.

- **Cost:** **$10,000** flat tuition fee (approx. ₹8.3 Lakhs).
- **Format:** 10 courses, 100% online, flexible completion schedule.
- **Requirements:** Similar to OMSCS (requires B.Tech CSE, GPA, Recommendations, 
  and TOEFL).

---

## 3. CU Boulder — MS in Computer Science (on Coursera)

The University of Colorado Boulder offers a unique, barrier-free pathway to a 
top-tier Master's degree hosted on Coursera.

- **Performance-Based Admission:** There is **no formal application process**. 
  You do not need to submit transcripts, GPAs, letters of recommendation, or TOEFL 
  scores.
- **The Pathway:** You enroll in 3 specific "pathway" courses on Coursera. If you 
  complete these courses with a **grade of B or better**, you are automatically 
  admitted to the full degree program.
- **Cost:** **$15,750** total tuition (approx. ₹13 Lakhs).

## Critical Realities: Migration & Visa Limitations

> [!WARNING]
> **No US Visas or OPT:** Online degrees from US universities **do not** qualify 
> international students for F-1 student visas, nor do they grant eligibility for 
> Optional Practical Training (OPT) or STEM extensions. 
> You cannot use these programs to migrate to the US directly.

### Equivalence in India (AIU Attestation)
For foreign degrees to be recognized for government jobs and PhD applications in 
India, they must receive an equivalence certificate from the **Association of 
Indian Universities (AIU)**. 
- AIU generally mandates that the foreign degree must involve a minimum period of 
  on-campus study. 
- Therefore, 100% online foreign degrees may not be certified by AIU for government 
  positions in India, though they are highly respected in the private tech sector 
  (e.g., product companies, startups, and MNCs).

## Strategic Recommendation for a TCS Software Engineer

For a 23-year-old B.Tech CSE graduate at TCS:
- **Excellent for Product Transition:** An OMSCS degree from Georgia Tech carries 
  immense weight in the private sector. It is the cheapest way to get an elite Ivy-league 
  equivalent US degree on your resume while earning a salary at TCS.
- **Forex Planning:** You will pay tuition course-by-course using a credit card or 
  forex card. Plan for currency fluctuations.
- **Time Commitment:** These are rigorous, regular US master's level programs. Expect 
  to spend **15–20 hours per week** studying. Make sure you can balance this with 
  your project deliveries at TCS.
