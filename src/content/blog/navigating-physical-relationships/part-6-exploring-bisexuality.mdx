# Exploring Bisexuality Safely and Confidently

You mentioned you think you like both men and women but aren't entirely sure. This is an incredibly common experience in your early 20s. You do not need to figure it all out immediately. 

## The Pressure to "Pick a Side"

As a bisexual man, you might face weird pressures from both straight and gay communities. Some straight people might view it as a phase, while some gay men might view you as "confused." Ignore them. Your attraction is valid, regardless of who you happen to be dating or sleeping with at any given moment.

## How to Explore Safely

* **Start Slow:** You don't have to jump into the deep end. Talk to other queer people. Join online communities (like Reddit's r/LGBTindia) to read about others' experiences.
* **Be Honest with Partners:** If you are with someone and it's your first time exploring that side of your sexuality, it is often best to just tell them. A good partner will be understanding and help guide the experience.
* **Protect Your Privacy:** India is evolving, but homophobia/biphobia still exists. You are allowed to be discreet. You do not owe anyone a "coming out" announcement until you are 100% ready and feel safe doing so.

## Avoid Fetishization

Sometimes, people looking for "experimentation" end up treating same-sex partners like secrets or objects rather than human beings. Remember that the men you interact with have feelings and boundaries too. Treat your same-sex encounters with the same respect and dignity as your heterosexual ones.
