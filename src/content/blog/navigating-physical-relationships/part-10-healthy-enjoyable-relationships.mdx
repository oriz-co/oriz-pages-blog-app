# Building Healthy, Enjoyable Physical Relationships

As a 23-year-old guy working at TCS in Bhubaneswar, exploring your bisexuality and figuring out life, you have a blank canvas ahead of you. 

Physical relationships—whether they are one-night stands, summer flings, or deep, long-term commitments—are a beautiful part of the human experience. But they are only enjoyable when built on the right foundation.

## The Ultimate Checklist for Your 20s

If you want to get the absolute most enjoyment out of your physical life:

1. **Be Honest:** With yourself and your partners. Don't play games.
2. **Be Safe:** Condoms and STI testing are non-negotiable. 
3. **Be Respectful:** Treat every partner, regardless of gender or the duration of the encounter, with basic human dignity.
4. **Be Open-Minded:** Allow yourself to explore your attractions without judgment. Take the pressure off yourself to figure it all out right now.
5. **Focus on Giving:** The best lovers are the ones who pay attention to their partner's pleasure. Selfishness in the bedroom translates to loneliness outside of it.

## Your Life is Just Starting

You don't need to manipulate anyone to have a good time. You just need to be a confident, respectful, and communicative person. 

Go on dates. Explore Bumble and Grindr safely. Have fun. Make mistakes, but learn from them. Protect your peace, protect your health, and treat the people you are intimate with as teammates in mutual enjoyment, not objects to be used. 

That is how you win in your 20s.
