# Casual Fun vs. Using People

In a previous thought, you mentioned wanting to take "advantage" of people to form your enjoyment. It is vital to rewire that mindset if you want to have a genuinely fulfilling life.

There is a massive difference between having casual fun and using people.

## What Using People Looks Like (Toxic)
* Lying about wanting a relationship just so they will sleep with you.
* Pressuring them when they are drunk or vulnerable.
* Ignoring their texts after you get what you want.
* Continuing to sleep with them when you know they are falling in love with you, and you aren't.

If you do these things, you aren't "winning" at life. You are just creating trauma, ruining your reputation, and guaranteeing that high-quality people will eventually avoid you.

## What Ethical Casual Fun Looks Like (Healthy)
* Being 100% upfront that you only want something casual.
* Ensuring enthusiastic, sober consent.
* Treating them with respect, kindness, and decency before, during, and after sex.
* Prioritizing their physical pleasure just as much as your own.

You can have incredibly wild, fun, and adventurous physical relationships without ever manipulating anyone. Treat your casual partners like friends you happen to be intimate with. Respect is the bare minimum.
