# Consent is Sexy (And Mandatory)

If you want to have a fulfilling physical life in your 20s, there is one non-negotiable skill you must master: Consent. 

Consent isn't just about avoiding doing something wrong; it is the key to actually having *good* physical experiences. When both people feel safe and respected, the experience is infinitely better.

## What Enthusiastic Consent Looks Like

Consent is not the absence of a "no." It is an enthusiastic "yes."

* **It is ongoing:** Just because someone agreed to come to your apartment does not mean they agreed to have sex. Just because they agreed to one act doesn't mean they agreed to another.
* **It is clear:** If someone is hesitating, looking away, or saying "I don't know," that is a no. You stop immediately. 
* **It can be withdrawn:** Anyone can change their mind at any time, even in the middle of things. 

## How to Ask for Consent

Many guys worry that asking ruins the mood. This is a myth. Asking shows confidence and respect, which is highly attractive.

Examples of how to check in:
* "Can I kiss you?"
* "Are you comfortable with this?"
* "Do you want to keep going?"
* "Tell me what you like."

## The Rule of Mutual Enjoyment

You mentioned previously wanting to find "enjoyment." The absolute best way to guarantee you enjoy a physical relationship is to ensure your partner is enjoying it just as much. If you are only focused on your own pleasure, you are being a bad partner, and word travels fast in social circles. Mutual pleasure is the goal.
