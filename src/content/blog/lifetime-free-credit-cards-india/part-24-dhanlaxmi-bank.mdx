# Part 24: Dhanlaxmi Bank Ltd. — Credit Card Offerings

**Dhanlaxmi Bank Ltd.** is an old private sector bank headquartered in Kerala. They have a very conservative approach to retail lending and credit cards.

---

## 🏦 Dhanlaxmi Bank's Credit Card Strategy

Unlike many peers who rely on co-branding (like City Union or DCB), Dhanlaxmi Bank does issue its own proprietary credit card, most notably the **Dhanlaxmi Bank Platinum Credit Card**.

---

## 💳 Do They Offer Lifetime Free Cards?

**Yes, but with heavy caveats.**

The Dhanlaxmi Bank Platinum Credit Card is marketed with a very appealing feature: **No Joining Fee and No Annual Fee.** It is technically a Lifetime Free (LTF) card.

### The Catch: Why You Shouldn't Get It
While the card is free, it is virtually useless for a modern consumer in 2026.
*   **Zero Reward Ecosystem:** The card offers practically no reward points, no cashback, and no milestone benefits.
*   **No Lifestyle Perks:** There is no airport lounge access, no movie ticket discounts, and no dining benefits.
*   **Strict Issuance:** It is generally only issued to existing customers who maintain a strong relationship with the bank in its core operating regions (primarily South India).

---

## 🎯 The TCS / Corporate Strategy

If you are a TCS employee, do not waste a CIBIL inquiry on this card. 

Even if you want a basic, no-frills Lifetime Free card, the **ICICI Platinum Chip Card** or the **Amazon Pay ICICI Card** offers infinitely better tech support, app UI, and merchant EMI offers.

*   **Verdict:** Ignore. A card being "Lifetime Free" is only valuable if the card actually provides a service or reward.
